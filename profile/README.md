## Overview

1Password Advanced is a trusted password and secrets management application that securely stores passwords, credit cards, identities, secure notes, and sensitive documents behind end-to-end encryption with seamless autofill across every browser and application. It eliminates the dangerous practice of reusing passwords or storing them in plaintext by making strong, unique credentials effortless to generate and use. The intuitive interface makes security accessible to everyone, from individuals managing personal accounts to teams securing shared infrastructure.

End-to-end encryption with a Secret Key architecture ensures that your data is encrypted before it leaves your device and can only be decrypted with your master password combined with your Secret Key. This dual-layer approach makes unauthorized access computationally impossible even if server data were compromised. Independent security audits verify the cryptographic implementation, and the zero-knowledge architecture means that even 1Password's own servers cannot read your stored data.

Browser integration autofills login credentials, credit cards, and identity information directly in web forms across Safari, Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. This makes secure credential management as convenient as the insecure practice of reusing passwords, removing the friction that causes people to abandon password managers. Inline suggestions appear contextually as you navigate the web, and new credentials are captured and saved automatically when you create accounts on new sites.

Watchtower security dashboard monitors your stored credentials against known data breaches, identifies weak or reused passwords, and flags accounts missing two-factor authentication. This provides a continuous security audit of your digital identity that highlights the most urgent vulnerabilities first. Watchtower also checks for expiring credit cards, unsecured HTTP logins, and compromised websites, creating a comprehensive security posture overview in a single view.

For anyone who wants their passwords and sensitive information stored with bank-grade encryption while remaining instantly accessible through autofill and cross-device sync, 1Password Advanced is the security tool that makes strong credential management effortless. Its combination of robust encryption, thoughtful interface design, and proactive security monitoring sets the standard for what a password manager should be.

## Performance

1Password balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
